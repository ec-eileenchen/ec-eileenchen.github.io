Empowering Our Community with Knowledge & Resources

This fraud detection and user protection project was designed with a specific group of people in mind, the clients and community served by the Financial Empowerment network (FEN). We recognize that individuals and families, especially those in minority and immigrant communities or those facing financial hardships, can be disproportionately affected by fraud and may have fewer resources to navigate the complex financial system when it occurs.


This webpage is a direct response to that need. It was created to be a trusted accessible, and empowering resource fopr anyone who wants to understand how their credit card security is protected and what to do if that security is breached,.


Who This is For

This resource is for you if:

  • You are worried and want to be prepared. You are a credit card user and want to understand how banks protect you and what your rights are, before anything goes wrong.
  • You are uncertain and is seeking clarity. You've received a fraud alert from your bank and felt uncertain or anxious. This tool demysifies the process, explaining in simple terms what might have triggered that alert.
  • You are facing a crisis and need a clear path. You have discovered an unauthorized transaction on your account and need to know the steps to take to protect your money and your identity.
  • You are seeking trustworthy information. You need clear, straightforward information with minimal banking terminology that is available in multuple languages, and is easy to access on a smartphone.

    • What This Tool Provides

    We bridge the gap between advanced banking technology and your sense of credit card security. This is not a fraud reporting tool, but an educational and guidance resource that provides:

    1. Transparency: We explain the "black box" of bank fraud detection. Using Explainable AI, we show you the most common factors that cause a bank flag a transaction. This knowledge reduces fear and replaces it with understanding.
    2. Clarity on Your Rights: We clearly outline your legal protections under laws like the Fair Credit Billing Act. You will learn about zero-liability policies, your right to dispute charges, and how to place fraud alerts on your credit report.
    3. Actionable Resources: We provide direct links and information for critical next steps, whether that's reporting identity theft to the official government website or contacting a local organization like FEN for personalized support.

    The Signle Most Important Takeaway

    The primary goal of this webpage is for you to walk away understanding the foundation of how fraud detection works and the rights you possess. When you understand the "why" behind a bank's actions and the "what" you are legally entitled to, you can face a potential or actual fraud situation with confidence instead of panic.


    How to Use This Resource

    The page is designed for easy navigation on any device.

  • Start with "Banks Suspicious Activity Detection" to satisfy your curiosity about fraud detection.
  • Go to "Your Credit Card Rights" to learn the most critical knowledge for your financial security.
  • Use "Support & Resources" when you need help or more guidance.
